2Acceptable use

The tool may only be used to:

  • Connect the operator's own TikTok account via OAuth.
  • Review videos produced by the operator before publishing.
  • Publish those videos to the connected account, at the privacy level the operator selects.

It must not be used to connect or publish on behalf of any account the operator does not own, to automate engagement (likes, follows, comments), or to circumvent any TikTok Community Guideline or Terms of Service.

3Your TikTok account

Connecting an account is done entirely through TikTok's own OAuth login — this tool never sees or stores a TikTok password. Access can be revoked at any time from TikTok's Settings → Manage apps and devices, which immediately stops the tool from publishing on that account's behalf.
